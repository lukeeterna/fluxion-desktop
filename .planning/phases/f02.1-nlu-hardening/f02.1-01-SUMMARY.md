## Decisions Made

- Modal + cambia/modifica requires booking object: original plan had modal pattern as unconditionally safe, but "devo cambiare le gomme" proved this wrong. Split into two modal patterns: unambiguous domain verbs (sposta/anticipa/posticipa) remain safe, cambia/modifica require booking object even with modal.
- _disambiguate_hour_pm applied ONLY in PHASE 5 (bare digit hour without minutes). PHASE 1 (explicit minutes "alle 3:30") correctly stays AM — heuristic not applied there.
- STT truncations added as simple dict entries alongside canonical forms — no regex complexity needed.
