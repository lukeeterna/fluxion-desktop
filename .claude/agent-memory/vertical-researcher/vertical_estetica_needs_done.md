Research completed 2026-03-31. Output: `.claude/cache/agents/vertical-needs-estetica.md`

Key findings:
- ~133.000 centri estetici + nail bar in Italy (target FLUXION: ~85.000 with 1-5 operators)
- 60% still on paper/Excel — biggest untapped segment
- Problem #1: "can't answer phone while in treatment cabin" — Sara is the exact solution
- No-show rate 12-18% = €16.000-20.000/year loss per center — reminders cut it 40%
- Fresha is free but takes 25% on new clients from marketplace — huge resentment
- Scheda cliente medica (controindicazioni, consenso GDPR) is legally required for machines — zero competitors do this well
- Cabin scheduling (not just staff) is a gap in all mainstream tools
- Before/after photo timeline with social consent flag is missing everywhere
- Seasonal demand: peak June-July (epilazione), Christmas (gift cards 15-25% of December revenue)
- ROI calc: FLUXION pays itself back in <1 month for a 200-appointment/month center

**Why:** Needed to validate P0 features before implementing estetica-specific scheda and Sara vertical config.
**How to apply:** Use ROI calc and "mani sporche di cera" framing in landing page copy for estetica segment.
